Ecodream: Discovery Project

Project Info

Definition of the analysis objectives and creation of a questionnaire and interview guide to be administered to the selected target audience. Development of personas based on the preliminary target analysis and the needs emerging from the research phase. Identification of possible use cases and scenarios for each persona, along with the mapping of their user journey across the current website, in order to uncover friction points and new opportunities for improvement. Presentation of an initial proposal for a reorganized site architecture, laying the groundwork for a more intuitive and user-centered navigation structure.
