Ecodream: Accessibility Project

Project Info

Creation of personas with disabilities, building on those developed in the Discovery project, each assigned one or more disability conditions relevant to website and device usage. Identification of use cases and mapping of the user journey across the current website for each persona, uncovering new opportunities for improvement. Selection of 5 pages from the analyzed website, and identification of accessibility issues based on established guidelines. For each issue, formulation of a proposed intervention to inform the subsequent redesign phase, concluding with an overall assessment of the accessibility challenges encountered.
