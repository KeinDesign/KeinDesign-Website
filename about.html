ABOUT

Let's get a bit more personal

The mountains have always
been my go-to place.

It's where I disconnect, recharge, and come back with a head full of new ideas. Climbing — both indoor and outdoor — is my way of staying grounded and pushing limits at the same time. It's not just a hobby; it's something that keeps me focused, curious, and constantly learning.

I first got into design when I realized how much I enjoyed making things cleaner, clearer, and more visually satisfying. That mix of structure and creativity? It just clicked for me. Since then, I've been on a journey to turn that instinct into meaningful, user-focused work.

My approach to Design

This is what I bring to the table. A few principles I stick to — no matter the project, the client, or the platform.

Empathy always leads
the way

Understanding users is where everything starts. I listen, observe, and put myself in their shoes to design experiences that actually work for real people.

Design is
smart problem-solving

To me, design isn't just about looks, it's about making things work better. Every project is a challenge I tackle with logic, curiosity and a splash of creativity.

Details make the
difference

From spacing to microinteractions, I obsess over the small stuff — because great experiences are built on precision and care.
